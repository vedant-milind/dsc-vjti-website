Welcome to Developer Students Club, VJTI!

We, at DSC VJTI, intend to build and enhance development skills and knowledge of students. It's a club where we learn and create amazing stuff together with other inquisitive developers and exhilarate everyone to their core. Our motto is to learn the necessary skills and collaborate and contribute to building solutions for real-world problems. Located in South Mumbai, VJTI is an autonomous institution owned by Maharashtra State Government. VJTI is known for its high-quality teaching, collaborative research, industry connect, and strong alumni network.

by Vedant Athavale, 27-11-2020
